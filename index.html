About Me!

I'm currently a second year Computer Science/ Advanced Maths student, and one of the CSESoc HR directors in 2024. If you've been involved with CSESoc this year, you would have seen me around the weekly BBQs and running internal bonding events. Alternatively, i've also run various external events for UNSW MathSoc over the past two years as Socials subcomm and Socials Director!!!

Throughout my experience with CSESoc, i've come to realise its remarkable capacity for helping form friendships and and connecting like minded individuals, ultimately facilitating a melding pot of our most talented individuals. The potential for growth within this community is unparalleled and I would love to, along with the 2025 exec team, nurture CSESoc to become more inclusive and beneficial for future CSE students!
