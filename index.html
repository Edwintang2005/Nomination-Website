About Me!

I'm currently a second year Computer Science/ Advanced Maths student, and one of the CSESoc HR directors in 2024. If you've been involved with CSESoc this year, you would have seen me around the weekly BBQs and running internal bonding events. Alternatively, i've also run various external events for UNSW MathSoc over the past two years as Socials subcomm and Socials Director!!!

Throughout my experience with CSESoc, i've come to realise its remarkable capacity for helping form friendships and and connecting like minded individuals, ultimately facilitating a melding pot of our most talented individuals. The potential for growth within this community is unparalleled and I would love to, along with the 2025 exec team, nurture CSESoc to become more inclusive and beneficial for future CSE students!

My vision

After some consideration, my goals for 2025 can be broken down into a few points!

Foster Inclusivity

  • Ensuring CSESoc's team has diverse representation by ensuring recruiting procedures are anonymised to reduce discrimination
  • Educating the team and raising awareness towards inclusive language and supportive practices to encourage an inclusive environment
  • Implementing strong feedback measures to regularly assess individual experiences with CSESoc and adjust for any negative experiences

    • Grow Alumini Network

  • Reach out to past and graduating Alumini for possible newsletter features and advice segments
  • Aim to run at least one Alumini event or discussion session a term to maintain connections
  • Invest in employment opportunities or training programs from Alumini

    • Bolster Academic Support

  • Create cheatsheats for all first year courses that contain theory revision and key command revision
  • Write and run revision seminars for all first year courses to boost first year confidence
  • Creating practice problems sets for all first year courses to provide practice beyond standard past papers

    • Create Engagement

  • Geater push for online and educational events, collaborating with adjacent societies to strengthen community presence
  • More engagement with sponsors, pushing for specific sponsor targeted career focused events
  • Targeted surveys for ideas and feedback from the community so we can implement broader visions
